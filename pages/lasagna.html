Lasagna Recipe

Description

Here is the recipe for a delicious Lasagna, a famous Italian layered pasta that everyone loves!

Ingredients

For this recipe we need the following:

  • Meat, preferably lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Two cloves of Garlic
  • Two cans of Tomato Sauce
  • Two tablespoons of Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Dried Basil Leaves
  • Salt and Italian Seasoning
  • Fennel Seeds and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. First, make the meat sauce
  2. Cook your noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna from bottom to top: Parmesan, Meat Sauce, Mozzarella Slices, Ricotta Mixture, Noodles, and Meat Sauce
  5. Repeat for each layer
  6. Cover with foil and bake and over preheated at around 375 degrees farenheit for 50 minutes
  7. Let rest before serving
  8. Enjoy!
